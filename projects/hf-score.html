House Rules Game variations
  • If you get stuck with negative cards in your hand, they remain negative.
  • You can have more than one book of the same number.
  • Take Cover: the end of the game changes; the ONLY way to end is to complete a clean book.
  • You can play an attack or action before reaching the minimum required points for the round.
  • Foot Pain: all players, including the person who played the card, skip the next round if they are in their foot.
  • If you have 5 cards of one number in your meld and a sixth in your hand, you can pick up a seventh from the discard pile. If you have 6 cards melded, you cannot pick up the seventh.
